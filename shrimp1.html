Sautéed Shrimp

Ingredients:

Shrimp

Turmeric, garlic powder, sea salt, black pepper, and paprika

Lemon

Butter

  1. Season the shrimp: Gently pat the shrimp dry with a paper towel to remove excess moisture. Place into a bowl, then add in all of the seasonings along with 1 tablespoon of oil.
  2. Cook, garnish, and serve: Heat additional oil in a skillet over medium heat, then add in shrimp. Cook 1-2 minutes per side turning with tongs (or a spatula if smaller shrimp is used), then squeeze lemon juice over the shrimp and toss gently to combine.
