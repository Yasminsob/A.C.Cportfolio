Oven-Baked Shrimp

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt + pepper, to taste
  • Chopped parsley or basil, to garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400F.
  2. Place all ingredients into a large baking dish, except for the garnish (parsley/basil). Generously season with salt and pepper, then toss everything together until shrimp is well coated in oil and seasonings.
  3. Arrange shrimp in a single layer, then place baking dish in the middle rack of your oven.
  4. Allow shrimp to cook for about 8-10 minutes. There's no need to flip them! The shrimp should be opaque and just cooked through, but you don't want them overdone or rubbery.
  5. Remove baking dish from oven, then garnish with chopped parsley or basil.
